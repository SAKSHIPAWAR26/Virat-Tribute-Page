About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Known for his aggressive batting style and consistent performances, Kohli has broken numerous records in international cricket.

Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, Kohli made his international debut in 2008 and quickly rose through the ranks to become one of the best batsmen in the world.

This tribute page celebrates his achievements and contributions to the sport of cricket.

Achievements

  • Fastest century by an Indian cricketer in ODIs
  • Most centuries in run chases in ODI cricket
  • Fastest batsman to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year: 2012, 2017, 2018
  • Padma Shri award, India's fourth-highest civilian award, in 2017
